High Performance Water Cooled Desktop Computer

Independently designed and assembled a high-performance personal computer (PC). This project required in-depth research to identify compatible components, understanding of hardware specifications for performance optimization, and meticulous assembly with proper cable management. Through this process, I developed strong problem-solving skills by troubleshooting compatibility issues and ensuring the system functioned as intended.
Highlights:

  • Prioritized components for gaming and machine learning/AI tasks to achieve a high-powered system that can do almost anything I need it to while keeping the components comfortably within thermal limits.
  • After studying the electronics and BIOS of the computer, I successfully overclocked the CPU and GPU for a large performance boost putting my system among the top performing of those with the same specification.
  • Implemented a custom watercooling solution including the CPU and GPU for improved thermal management and overclockability. This is monitored by an inline flow rate/temperature sensor
  • Distribution Plate with pump to allow for high flow rate of water and easier tube routing
